Best Budget Back to School Products

by Budget Tech Team

Posted on October 31, 2016

Going back to the classroom? Here we've listed 5 products under $60 to help you organize and excel in your academic careers.

#1:Rocketbook Wave Smart Notebook by Rocketbook ($25)

The Rocketbook allows students to quickly save all of their notes directly to the cloud, with their mobile device. Once a student has filled their notebook, using the included Pilot FriXion pen, with notes, drawings, etc., they can microwave the Smart Notebook and come back with a fresh set of empty pages. This item is an essential piece for the tech-enthusiast student, as it combines utility with style.

#2:Blockhead Side-Facing Plug for Apple Adapters ($19.95)

The Blockhead adapter allows Mac wall chargers to sit flush against the wall, opposed to its usual hazardous position. This item is very useful for those looking to clean up their setup, or help place plugs in tight spaces.

#3:Cocoon GRID-IT! Organizer Case ($11.95)

The GRID-IT Organizer is an object retention system for gadget organization, perfect for mobile accesories. Items are hold securely and safely, with an endless amount of configurations for most uses. This product is easy to slip into a backpack or other carrying case, and can organize countless other essential daily cables.

#4:Seagate Expansion 1TB Portable External Hard Drive USB 3.0 ($54.99)

Storage is necessary for most students going back to school. With the Seagate 1TB Hard Drive, you are getting fast data transfers, an excessive amount of space for the average user, and an affordable price.

#5:HYDRA SmartBottle ($59.99)

Though this waterbottle may be a little expensive at first glance, this product is on this list for good reason Included in this $59 waterbottle, you get a holistic package containing a water bottle, a bluetooth speaker with speakerphone, a 4000 mAh Power Bank Charger, and a Multicolor LED Light
