Best Budget Tech Gifts for the Holidays

by Budget Tech Team

Posted on November 1, 2016

Getting ready for the holiday season? Find the best budget tech gifts under $50 sure to please your significant other!

#1: Skullcandy Ink'd Bluetooth Wireless Earbuds ($37.99)

The Skullcandy Ink'd Earbuds are a low-profile set of lightweight bluetooth earphones that offer decent sound, noise isolation, and easy storage. An excellent present for any iPhone 7 owner, or any active athlete.

#2: Roku Express ($29.99)

The Roku Express is a quick access streaming alternative to set-top boxes at an affordable price. Without monthly rental fees, users have access to 1080p video from over 350,000+ movies and TV episodes across 3,500+ paid or free channels, including Netflix, Amazon Video, Hulu, PBS KIDS, and more. It even includes a remote! For this price, one cannot ask for much more.

#3: Native Union KEY Cable ($14.99)

Help someone never forget to bring their charger again! The Native Unione Key cable is a small stylish lightning cable that fits secure onto a key ring. It is Apple MFI certified so one does not need to worry this will stop working after an update. This item blends utility with functionality all for a low price, making this a supreme option for any holiday gift.

#4: Buckshot 2.0 Rugged Bluetooth Speaker ($39.95)

For the outdoorsy type in your life, the new and improved Buckshot 2.0 Bluetooth speaker is a perfect buy. This speaker can be securely strapped on to the handlebar of a bike, is ultra-portable, waterproof, shockproof, a 60-foot Bluetooth range, a 20-hour battery life, and provides decently loud sound quality.

#5: Native Union DOCK for iPhone or iPad ($49.99)

Although this product edges on the more premium end docks, it is a modern blend of premium materials and simplistic design, ideal for a holiday gift. This dock is made of premium weighted silicone combined with precision cut aluminum that matches most iPhone design and material.

#6: Microsoft Universal Foldable Keyboard ($50.98)

The Microsoft Universal Foldable KEyboard is an ultra-thin and lightweight bluetooth keyboard that easily fits into most purses and bags. It is designed to work with iPad, iPhone, Android, Windows tablets, and Windows Phone, but could easily become a living room essential, connecting to a TV or a game console.
